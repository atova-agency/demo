Core Emotional Spectrum
Purpose & Foundation
Purpose: Primary emotion definitions with physiological correlates
Why: Foundation for all emotional translation
emotion-baseline
Anxiety
Definition: Anticipation of future threat or negative outcome
Physiological: chest-tightness + rapid-heartbeat + muscle-tension
Triggers: uncertainty . perceived-danger . upcoming-events
Translation: detect chest tightness and rapid heartbeat - anxiety patterns
Anger
Definition: Response to perceived wrong, injustice, or frustration
Physiological: face-flushing + jaw-tension + increased-body-temperature
Triggers: blocked-goals . unfair-treatment . boundary-violation
Translation: experiencing face flushing and jaw tension - anger response
Sadness
Definition: Response to loss, disappointment, or unmet needs
Physiological: heavy-chest + slow-movement + low-energy
Triggers: loss . disappointment . helplessness
Translation: systems show heavy chest and low energy - sadness patterns
Joy-Happiness
Definition: Positive response to meaningful events or general contentment
Physiological: energy-surge + smiling + light-chest-sensation
Triggers: achievement . connection . needs-met
Translation: detect energy surge and smiling reflex - joy/happiness indicators
