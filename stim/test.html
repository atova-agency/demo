Adult Autism Screening Questionnaire

Important Disclaimer: This questionnaire is for informational purposes only and is not a diagnostic tool. Only a qualified healthcare professional can provide an accurate diagnosis of autism spectrum disorder. This screening can help you understand whether you might want to seek a formal evaluation.

Social Communication & Interaction

1. Do you find it challenging to interpret nonverbal cues like facial expressions, body language, or tone of voice?
2. Do you struggle with back-and-forth conversation or knowing when to speak in social situations?
3. Do you find it difficult to make or maintain friendships?
4. Do you prefer solitary activities over group activities?
5. Do you find small talk difficult or pointless?

Restricted & Repetitive Behaviors

6. Do you have strong, highly focused interests that you spend a lot of time on?
7. Do you feel distressed by changes in routine or unexpected events?
8. Do you engage in repetitive movements (like rocking, hand-flapping, or pacing) especially when excited or stressed?
9. Do you have rigid thinking patterns or difficulty adapting to new ways of doing things?
10. Do you have specific rituals or routines that you feel compelled to follow?

Sensory Sensitivities

11. Are you unusually sensitive to sounds, lights, textures, or smells?
12. Do you seek out specific sensory experiences (like certain textures, movements, or visual patterns)?
13. Do you become overwhelmed in busy environments like shopping malls or crowded parties?
14. Do you have unusual responses to pain, temperature, or other physical sensations?
15. Do you avoid certain foods because of their texture rather than taste?

Additional Traits & Experiences

16. Do you tend to take language very literally and struggle with sarcasm or metaphors?
17. Do you have difficulty recognizing or describing your own emotions?
18. Do you have a strong sense of justice or become very upset by unfairness?
19. Do you frequently feel like you're "masking" or pretending to be neurotypical in social situations?
20. Have you always felt different from others, even if you couldn't explain why?
