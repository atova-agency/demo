The "Sense-Reset-Go" Emergency Protocol

This is a three-step physical loop.

1

SENSE (External Anchor)

ACTION: Choose one sense and perform one action to engage it.

  • TOUCH: Press your palm firmly against any surface. Feel the pressure.
  • SOUND: Hum a single, low note. Feel the vibration in your chest.
  • SIGHT: Blink once, slowly. Notice the change in light.

GOAL: To get one piece of sensory data from outside your body.

2

RESET (Physiological Interrupt)

ACTION: Make your exhale longer than your inhale.

  • Don't count. Just push all the air out of your lungs in one slow, steady stream.
  • Feel your chest or shoulders relax as you empty your lungs.

GOAL: To trigger a physical reset in your nervous system.

3

GO (Micro-Momentum)

ACTION: Execute one tiny, predefined physical movement.

  • Examples: Nod your head "yes," tap your finger once, shift your weight, open and close your hand.

GOAL: To create the first spark of voluntary motion.

LOOP: If still stuck, repeat with a different sense and a different movement.
