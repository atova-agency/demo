Operational Protocol

Scan Phase

Rapid threat and asset assessment. Mandatory pre-Pact analysis.

  • Visual Sweep: Process the Tango and its environment.
  • Situation: Define the challenge in one clear sentence.
  • Obstacles: Identify primary blockers.
  • Assets: Inventory available tools, skills, and information.
  • Critical Path: Strip to essentials. Identify the core action sequence.
  • Threat Assessment: Evaluate failure modes and contingency triggers.

Time limit: <30 seconds.
