What is VoiceOver?

VoiceOver is a groundbreaking accessibility feature built into Apple's iOS that provides auditory descriptions of what's happening on your screen. It transforms visual information into spoken feedback, allowing users with visual impairments to fully interact with their iPhones and iPads.

Unlike simple screen readers, VoiceOver offers a gesture-based navigation system that lets you:

Touch Exploration

Drag your finger to hear what's under it without activating anything

Gesture Navigation

Use simple swipes and taps to navigate efficiently

Rotor Control

Quickly jump between headings, links, and other elements

Multi-Finger Gestures

Use multiple fingers for advanced navigation and commands
